summary()

Checks the need of "summarize" the string. When it is needed, the string is splitted. Then the last dot is removed and the terminator is pushed into the resultant string.

Measures the space needed by the terminator. Let‘s say you want a string with 50 chars, and your terminator is a ’…’. That means your string can only have 47 chars + 3 chars from your terminator.
